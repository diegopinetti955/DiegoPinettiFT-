About Diego

I am a certified fitness trainer with a solid academic foundation in Sports Science and several years of professional experience across Italy and the Netherlands. My background includes strength and endurance programming, functional training, CrossFit, mobility development, sport-specific preparation, and post-injury rehabilitation.

What truly drives me is helping people move better, feel stronger, and build confidence in what their body can achieve. Whether your goal is fat loss, strength development, improved performance, or recovery after an injury, I design tailored programs that are safe, effective, and sustainable.

Over the past years I’ve worked with a wide variety of clients — from complete beginners to competitive athletes and seniors. This experience has taught me that every person’s journey is unique, and that the key to long-term progress is a balance of evidence-based methods, consistency, and motivation.

My approach is straightforward: clear coaching, structured plans, and constant support. Training is not only about improving physical performance, but also about gaining resilience and confidence that carry over into daily life.

If you are ready to challenge yourself, I’ll be there to guide you every step of the way.
